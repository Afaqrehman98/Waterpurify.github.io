Who are we?

We are Purify, a water recycling plant that aims to address the global water crisis by effectively using and recycling drainage, sewerage, and unused water through a series of rigorous treatment processes. Our goal is to provide clean and safe water for agriculture, production, public spaces, and industrial purposes. We are based in Pakistan and are dedicated to implementing the United Nations' 6th Sustainable Development Goal for clean water and sanitation by recycling 40% of Pakistan's water by 2030. Our team is made up of experts in water treatment, engineering, and marketing, who are committed to finding sustainable solutions to the water crisis. We are dedicated to making a positive impact on the environment and the communities we serve.

What services we provide?

We offer the largest selection of Water purifying services.

Water Treatment

This service would involve using different techniques, such as filtration, chemical treatment, and reverse osmosis, to purify water for various industrial, agricultural, and commercial uses.

Sewage Treatment

This service would involve treating and purifying wastewater to make it safe for reuse and discharge into the environment.

Desalination

This service would involve removing salt and other minerals from seawater to make it suitable for drinking and other uses.

Water Recycling

This service would involve collecting, treating, and reusing greywater and other types of waste water for irrigation, industrial processes, and other non-potable uses.

Water Testing and Analysis

This service would involve conducting lab tests to determine the quality and safety of water, as well as identifying any contaminants or pollutants that may be present.

Water Conservation

This service would involve providing advice and solutions to help individuals, businesses, and organizations reduce their water consumption and waste, through efficient usage and rainwater harvesting.
